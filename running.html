About Me

Hello! I'm William Plunkett, a proud resident of Boylston passionate about our community's future. I’m running for Select Board to bring fresh ideas and represent all voices, especially youth, in town governance.

My Platform

  • Support for LGBTQ+ Rights and Inclusion: I am dedicated to making Boylston a welcoming and safe community for everyone. This means supporting policies that protect against discrimination and celebrating diversity.
  • Reproductive Rights: While state and federal laws govern these issues, I support access to local health resources and education that respect personal choices.
  • Welcoming Immigrants and New Residents: Boylston should be a town that embraces newcomers and values the richness that diversity brings to our community.
  • Support for Transgender Rights: I stand against discrimination and support equal opportunities for transgender individuals, including respect and acceptance in all areas of life.
  • Environmental Action: I will advocate for local policies that protect our environment, promote sustainability, and align with global efforts like the Paris Climate Agreement.
